## Pakistan's Open Source Footprint

Over the past three years, Pakistan's contribution to global open source has grown significantly. Here's what the data shows.

### Contribution Trends

GitHub data from 2024–2026 reveals:

- **45% increase** in Pakistani developers contributing to open source
- **Top projects**: TensorFlow, Kubernetes, VS Code, Homebrew
- **Average contributor age**: 22 (median)

### Notable Pakistani Projects

1. **Roshan语音** — Urdu NLP toolkit (2.8k stars)
2. **PakElect** — Election monitoring framework (1.2k stars)
3. **Dastaan** — A platform for preserving oral histories

### The Gap We're Bridging

Despite this growth, most contributions remain individual efforts. There's no structured pipeline connecting university talent to meaningful open-source work. That's exactly what Prime Innovators is building.

The potential is enormous — we just need the infrastructure.
